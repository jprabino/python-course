Operaciones sobre Secuencias

Operation Result
x in s True if an item of s is equal to x, else False
x not in s False if an item of s is equal to x, else True
s + t the concatenation of s and t
s * n or n * s equivalent to adding s to itself n times
s[i] ith item of s, origin 0
s[i:j] slice of s from i to j
s[i:j:k] slice of s from i to j with step k
len(s) length of s
min(s) smallest item of s
max(s) largest item of s
s.index(x[, i[, j]]) index of the first occurrence of x in s (at or after index i and before index j)
s.count(x) total number of occurrences of x in s

Operaciones sobre Secuencias Mutables

Operation Result Operation Result
s[i] = x item i of s is replaced by x s.copy() creates a shallow copy of s (same as s[:])
s[i:j] = t slice of s from i to j is replaced by the contents of the iterable ts.extend(t) or s += t extends s with the contents of t (for the most part the same as s[len(s):len(s)] = t)
del s[i:j] same as s[i:j] = [] s *= n updates s with its contents repeated n times
s[i:j:k] = t the elements of s[i:j:k] are replaced by those of t s.insert(i, x) inserts x into s at the index given by i (same as s[i:i] = [x])
del s[i:j:k] removes the elements of s[i:j:k] from the lists.pop([i]) retrieves the item at i and also removes it from s
s.append(x) appends x to the end of the sequence (same as s[len(s):len(s)] = [x]) s.remove(x) remove the first item from s where s[i] == x
s.clear() removes all items from s (same as del s[:]) s.reverse() reverses the items of s in place

Sintaxis

'Indentacion'

En Python no hay caracteres que separen los bloques de código, ya sea para definicion de funciones, clases o control de flujo.

Los bloques de código son separados por la tabulacion/sangria o "indentación"

La recomendación es utilizar 4 espacios por "indentación".

Declaración de funciones

  • Se pueden poner cualquier cantidad de argumentos
  • Puede haber argumentos "opcionales" que tienen valores por defecto
  • En Python todo es un objeto, con lo cual los pasajes son siempre por referencia.
